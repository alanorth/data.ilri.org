Data resources at ILRI

Information about ILRI's data, tools, and code.

Data

The ILRI Data Portal makes ILRI's open access datasets globally available. The platform is also the main access point for data from the CGIAR Research Program on Livestock and Fish (CRP) and from other CGIAR research programs and centres as part of our cross-centre collaboration.

Tools

The Tool Portal provides metadata and documentation for a wide range of research tools used by the CGIAR and our partners. It also enables interaction among users and offers a platform to feedback on adaptation and use of the tools.
